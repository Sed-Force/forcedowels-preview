Frequently Asked Questions

Patent Pending Technology

General

What are Force Dowels™?
Force Dowels™ are patent-pending, high-strength cabinetry fasteners designed to deliver a clean exterior surface with no visible screws, faster assembly, and a premium finish.
Do they replace traditional dowels or screws?
Yes—Force Dowels are designed to replace standard 8 mm wood dowels and many screw applications in RTA and shop assembly workflows.
Are Force Dowels reusable?
No. They’re intended for permanent, production-grade assemblies for consistent strength and finish.
Do I need glue?
No glue is required. Eliminating glue removes cure time and speeds throughput while maintaining strong joints.

Compatibility & Installation

What hole size do I need?
Force Dowels are designed for **8 mm** dowel bores commonly used in cabinet manufacturing. Use standard 32 mm-system boring equipment or CNC programs.
Do they work with standard RTA lines?
Yes. They integrate seamlessly with most 8 mm dowel systems and RTA processes with little to no program changes.
What tools are required?
Standard dowel insertion or hand press equipment works. A simple fixture or clamp is recommended for consistent results.
Can I disassemble after installation?
Force Dowels are designed for permanent assemblies. If a reversible joint is required, contact us for guidance.
